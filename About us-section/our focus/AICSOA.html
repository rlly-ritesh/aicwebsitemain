Atal Innovation Mission

The Atal Innovation Mission (AIM), launched by the Government of India in 2016 under NITI Aayog, aims to foster innovation and entrepreneurship nationwide. As part of this mission, the Atal Incubation Centre (AIC) initiative supports technology-driven startups across the country.

Siksha 'O' Anusandhan Deemed to be University (SOADU) in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, has been selected to establish an AIC, named AIC-SOA Foundation. This center will receive Grant-in-Aid over five years, acknowledging its potential to drive innovation.
