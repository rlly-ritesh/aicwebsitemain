Transforming Business

PLANTERY

Plantery has developed innovative plant-based batteries using grass, eliminating toxic chemicals. The startup created 15+ prototypes, partnered with Indian Oil (₹29.5 lakh grant), attracted Shell Global USA interest, won 14+ awards, gained IIT support, and ranks among India's top 5 green solution startups.

driEV

DriEV is a dynamic venture that fuses innovation, sustainability, and local manufacturing to reshape the future of campus and urban commuting. The startup has been showcased at ICAI-Startup Sphere'23 and exhibited at Aarogya Manthan.

EMINENT PRODUCTS

The cutting-edge innovation focuses on developing a compact, high-torque gearbox with precision power transmission. This technology is aimed at meeting the demands of the rapidly growing automation market across sectors such as defense, robotics, electric vehicles (EVs), AGVs, medical devices, and aerospace.

PHOTOSPIMEDX PVT. LTD.

A Start-up developing a smartphone-based fluorescence Spectroscopic device for early stage cervical and oral cancer diagnosis, with the business model being a combination of offline and online market research. Their ultimate goal is to design and develop affordable, sensitive, specific, user friendly, rapid and robust, equipment free and accessible products.

EVSPEER

EVSPeer works on EV charging infrastructure and energy storage with products like Charz (static EV level 1 charging unit) and Anerzy (portable EV charger with battery backup). The company has sold over 20 Charz units and has 438 subscribers for Anerzy.

GREEN MANDI DEALS PVT. LTD.

Green Mandi is developing a tech-driven ERP platform designed to connect farmers, traders, SMEs, and buyers through a secure, unified B2B marketplace. Their solution aims to digitize the unorganized agri-trade sector, enabling trust, transparency, and seamless transactions across the entire agricultural value chain.

SPK-MAK TECHNOLOGIES

SPK-MAK Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is a forward-thinking tech company offering end-to-end electronic product engineering and ODM solutions. We help startups, enterprises, and institutions turn ideas into market-ready, smart, and cost-effective products. Innovation is our culture — combining Robotics, Embedded Systems, IoT, AI/ML, and CAD to build future-ready solutions for real-world challenges

HYDROVALLEY

Hydro Valley is transforming how Eastern India eats with fresh, nutrient-rich, and pesticide-free produce, straight from Bhubaneswar’s first-ever indoor commercial hydroponic farm. Leading the way in sustainable, soil-free agriculture, we grow each leaf in a carefully controlled environment using only RO-purified water and essential nutrients Our approach to sustainable farming minimizes water usage, eliminates the need for pesticides, and reduces our carbon footprint, providing clean, vibrant food that’s as kind to the planet as it is to you.

AngioSense

AngioSense is a non-invasive diagnostic device designed to detect blood vessel blockages without radiation or contrast dyes. Using sensors for SpO₂, temperature, pulse, and bioimpedance, it monitors physiological changes linked to vascular issues. A compact microcontroller processes this data to identify blockage patterns, such as reduced oxygen saturation, cooler skin temperature, and altered tissue resistance, enabling safer, quicker, and side-effect-free vascular health assessment.

AXHA Bioenergy Private Limited

AXHA Bioenergy Private Limited is an emerging startup in the renewable energy sector, focused on innovative biofuel manufacturing solutions. The company’s state-of-the-art, multipurpose production facilities are engineered to process a diverse range of feedstocks—including acid oil, non-edible oils, animal fats, and used cooking oils—linenabg the efficient production of high-quality, eco-friendly biodiesel.

Study Tours India

Study Tours India provides experiential learning programs like Industrial and Cultural Trips, Awarenes Campaigns, Career Guidance, Meaningful and Focused Workshops to students and interested individuals aiming to develop creative and qualitative skills of an individual.

Urban Hyphae

Urban Hyphae is revolutionizing fashion with sustainable, mushroom-based leather alternatives. Combining beauty, durability, and eco-conscious design, their cruelty-free materials avoid harmful chemicals and cut water use compared to animal leather. By harnessing fungi’s natural properties, the startup addresses environmental concerns while inspiring a shift toward ethical, responsible fashion where sustainability and style coexist seamlessly.

PowerBox Pvt. Ltd

PowerBox Pvt. Ltd offers a Battery-as-a-Service (BaaS) model that makes power storage affordable and flexible. Instead of buying batteries, users can subscribe for solar systems, home inverters, or EVs, lowering upfront costs. The plan includes maintenance, upgrades, and replacements, ensuring hassle-free use and scalability. PowerBox simplifies clean energy adoption, making it more accessible and cost-effective for households and electric vehicle users.

Smart Bloom

SmartBloom is transforming vertical farming with an automated cross-pollination system that fuses robotics, machine learning, and agricultural science. Built on open-source platforms like Arduino and Edge Impulse, the solution enhances efficiency, yield, and precision while reducing labor and errors. Designed for scalability, this cost-effective system offers a sustainable alternative for modern agriculture, particularly in controlled environments such as indoor farms and greenhouses.

ParQ Pvt. Ltd.

ParQ Pvt. Ltd. is a smart parking platform that simplifies urban mobility by connecting drivers with real-time, bookable spots via a mobile app and in-car integration. It lets users locate and reserve parking in advance, saving time and fuel. For operators, ParQ provides digital dashboards, automated monitoring, and analytics to optimize efficiency, cut costs, and digitize infrastructure for smarter, sustainable parking.

Healthligence

We are building an EHR from the ground up with a Patient-Centred, Medico-First philosophy. Designed for intuitive use, it enables doctors to access patient data quickly through AI-driven interactions, ensuring nothing is missed. With LLM and machine learning integration, it supports progress analysis, real-time collaboration, and efficient sharing of reports in a cloud-native or self-hosted environment, empowering medicos to focus more on patient care.

FRIDAY EVENTS PVT LTD - EINVIE

EINVIE addresses fragmented vendor visibility, inefficient event planning, lack of standardization, and challenges in connecting users with quality services for seamless event management. EINVIE is an innovative event management platform designed to simplify event planning and empower vendors. It connects users with talented vendors for private and public events, offering features like e-invitations, guest management, live updates, and ticketing.
